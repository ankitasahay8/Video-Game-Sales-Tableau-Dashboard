# 🎮 Video Game Sales Analysis Dashboard (Tableau)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an **interactive Tableau dashboard** analyzing global video game sales data.
The goal of this project is to explore sales trends, identify the most popular game genres and platforms, and highlight the top-performing video games worldwide.

The dashboard helps understand how the gaming industry has evolved over time and which factors contribute most to game sales.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1️⃣ Sales Trend

Shows the **global video game sales trend over the years**, helping identify periods of high and low sales.

### 2️⃣ Sales by Genre

Visualizes which **game genres generate the highest sales**, such as Action, Sports, or Shooter.

### 3️⃣ Sales by Platform

Displays **platform-wise sales performance**, highlighting which gaming consoles contributed most to the industry.

### 4️⃣ Top 10 Games

Highlights the **top-selling video games globally**, allowing quick identification of the most successful titles.

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

The dataset contains information about video game sales across different regions and platforms.

Key attributes include:

* Game Name
* Platform
* Genre
* Publisher
* Year of Release
* North America Sales
* Europe Sales
* Japan Sales
* Other Region Sales
* Global Sales

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* **Action and Sports genres dominate global video game sales.**
* Certain platforms like **Wii and PlayStation** contributed significantly to overall game sales.
* Games such as **Wii Sports** and **Super Mario Bros.** are among the highest-selling video games.
